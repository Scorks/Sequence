SEQUENCE

What is Sequence?

Sequence is a board game invented by Douglas Reuter in Minneapolis, Minnesota, over a two-year period in the 1970's. The game was originally called "Sequence Five". Reuter spent years developing the concept for the game, and in June of 1981, granted Jax Ltd. an exclusive license to manufacture and distribute the board game. The first game was sold in 1982.


Intended for use by the students and faculty of Memorial University of Newfoundland.
